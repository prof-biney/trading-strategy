## Overview

This is a fully mechanical, top-down strategy built on Smart Money Concepts (SMC). Every trade requires all conditions to be met before execution. No discretion, no signals, no exceptions.

---

## Step 1 — Top-Down Bias (Daily & HTF)

*Apply the Double Bias / Simi Theory framework before anything else.*

- [ ] Mark Daily highs, lows, and key liquidity pools (double tops/bottoms, equal highs/lows, trendlines)
- [ ] Mark FVGs, imbalances, and premium/discount zones on the Daily chart
- [ ] Identify the **Daily target** — the draw on liquidity price is likely heading toward
- [ ] Confirm bias alignment on **4H and 1H** (HTF directional bias)
- [ ] **Only trade in the direction of this bias.** No counter-bias trades, ever.

---

## Step 2 — Markout Plays & Setup Identification

*Identify what's next. Mark it out. Set alerts. Step away.*

- [ ] Mark orderblocks, mitigations, and refinements from **H4 down to M15**
- [ ] Identify the nearest untapped OBs, FVGs, and liquidity above/below current price
- [ ] Draw the potential play on TradingView — entry zone, target, invalidation
- [ ] Set price alerts at key zones
- [ ] **Do not sit and watch charts.** Let price come to you.

---

## Step 3 — Entry Criteria

> ⚠️ **All three conditions must be true before executing any trade.**

### 3a — Price is at Discount (buys) or Premium (sells)

- Draw a Fibonacci retracement on the **most recent swing** (swing low → high for buys; swing high → low for sells)
- Price must be at or **below 50% Fib** — minimum discount zone for buys
- Price at or **below 25% Fib** — premium entry, stronger confluence, preferred
- **Do not enter a buy above 50% Fib. Do not enter a sell below 50% Fib.**

### 3b — M1 OB 2.0 Confirmation is Present

- Drop to the **M1 chart** and wait for an OB 2.0 pattern to form at or near the entry zone
- The OB must align with the **HTF bias direction** — no counter-bias confirmations
- **This is your trigger.** No OB 2.0 on M1 = no trade, regardless of how good the setup looks

### 3c — Risk Parameters Check

- SL must be **≤ 40 pips** — if structure requires a wider stop, skip the trade
- TP must be **≥ 60 pips** — minimum 1:1.5 RR, targeting the Daily liquidity draw
- Set SL alert and BE alert **before executing** — no exceptions
- Size your lot so that the SL is less than **0.5% of your account** (or your defined max risk per trade)

## Step 4 — Trade Management

*After entry, follow this exactly. No improvising.*

1. **Immediately** set SL alert at your stop loss level
2. Set **BE alert at +40 pips** — when triggered, manually move SL to entry price
3. At **80–100 pips profit**, close 50–75% of the position (major scale-out)
4. Let the **runner** continue toward the Daily target with SL at breakeven
5. Mark all potential levels ahead of price — **do not move TP prematurely**
6. You may close the runner manually **only if price shows structural rejection** at a key level before TP — this must be a rule-based reason, not a feeling

## Hard Rules — Non-Negotiable

### 🚫 No news trading
Check FxFactory **before every session**. No entries within 30 minutes before or after any high-impact USD or XAU news event. If news falls within the session window, skip that window entirely.

### 🚫 Session only
All trades must be **entered between 05:30 – 09:00 GMT**. No trades outside this window, even if a setup looks perfect.

### 🚫 3 trades maximum per day
Once 3 trades are taken — win or lose — the session is **closed**. Step away. No revenge trades. No "one more."

### 🚫 No external signals, ever
You trade your own setups only. Following signals invalidates your edge data, breaks your process, and removes your ability to build real conviction in your system.
